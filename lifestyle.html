Lifestyle

Barefoot Blonde is a personal blog that was started by Amber
Fillerup Clark to be a place where she could document
her life, like a diary. She initially had the goal of
inspiring just a handful of people with her content,
but now the blog has grown into one of the biggest Lifestyle
blogs on the internet. On her blog, Amber shares a mixture
of lifestyle, beauty, and fashion content for her thousands of readers.

Her popular posts are usually her outfits where she shows her beautifully
curated style, her hair tutorials, and the inspiring adventures that she
goes on with her family.
If you’re looking for a positive and inspiring personal blog to read,
Barefoot Blonde is great.
If you enjoy Amber’s content, she even has her own hair care line, Dae,
which has also been a big hit!
